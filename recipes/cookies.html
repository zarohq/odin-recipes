A perfect recipe to perfect cookies

follow these steps!

Ingredients...

  • ½ cup granulated sugar(100 g)
  • ¾ cup brown sugar(165 g), packed
  • ½ cup unsalted butter(115 g), melted
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour(155 g)
  • 4 oz milk or semi-sweet chocolate chunks(110 g)

The process...

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the sugars, salt, and butter until a paste forms with no lumps.
  2. Whisk in the egg and vanilla, beating until light ribbons fall off the whisk and remain for a short while before falling back into the mixture.
  3. Fold in the chocolate chunks, then chill the dough for at least 30 minutes. For a more intense toffee-like flavor and deeper color, chill the dough overnight. The longer the dough rests, the more complex its flavor will be.
  4. Scoop the dough with an ice-cream scoop onto a parchment paper-lined baking sheet, leaving at least 4 inches (10 cm) of space between cookies and 2 inches (5 cm) of space from the edges of the pan so that the cookies can spread evenly.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges have started to barely brown.
